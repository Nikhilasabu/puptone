Write for Us

We are always on the lookout for new expert voices to join our team of recipe developers and writers. Please submit pitches along with three examples of your published work (print or online) and a brief bio to write@puptone.com. We accept pitches for pet care, how-tos, tips, and technique-driven posts. We do not accept pitches for travel writing, pet reviews, or pet out stories. Please review our site before pitching us. Please note that we do not accept unsolicited guest posts or articles.
