Popular Dogs

Golden Retriever

The Golden Retriever is a Scottish breed of retriever dog of medium size. It is characterised by a gentle and affectionate nature and a striking golden coat.

Labrador

The Labrador Retriever or simply Labrador, is a British breed of retriever gun dog.The Labrador is friendly, energetic, and playful.

Beagle

The beagle is a breed of small scent hound, similar in appearance to the much larger foxhound. The beagle was developed primarily for hunting hare, known as beagling.

Poodle

The Poodle, called the Pudel in German and the Caniche in French, is a breed of water dog. The breed is divided into four varieties based on size, the Standard Poodle, Medium Poodle, Miniature Poodle and Toy Poodle, although the Medium Poodle is not universally recognised.

German Shepherd

The German Shepherd is a German breed of working dog of medium to large size.It has since been used in many other types of work, including disability assistance, search-and-rescue, police work, and warfare.

Rottweiler

The Rottweiler is a breed of domestic dog, regarded as medium-to-large or large.They are well known to be powerful, protective, and loyal, famous for their instinct for guarding.

Doberman

They're highly energetic and smart dogs who perform well in police and military work, canine sports, and as family guardians and companions.
